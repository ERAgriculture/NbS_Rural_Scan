# Rural NbS Scan
**World Bank consultancy | CIAT / Alliance of Bioversity International**
*Procurement No. 0002016084*

Spatial prioritisation evidence base for Nature-based Solutions (NbS) in rural agriculture and forestry contexts, targeting Least Developed Countries (LDCs).

---

## Project phases

| Phase | Description |
|-------|-------------|
| 1 — Inception | Problem framing, scope definition, stakeholder alignment |
| 2 — Stocktake & Benchmarking | Systematic evidence review of NbS spatial methods (peer-reviewed + grey literature) |
| 3 — Methodology Development | Design of an integrated NbS spatial prioritisation framework |
| 4 — Piloting | Application and validation in selected country contexts |

---

## Scope: 10 priority NbS practices

**Forest & Tree Systems**
- Forest conservation and restoration
- Reforestation & afforestation
- Community-based forest management
- Agroforestry
- Agrosilvopastoralism / silvopastoralism

**Water & Wetland Systems**
- Wetland management
- Constructed wetlands
- Riparian buffers
- Water harvesting and conservation

**Managed Landscapes**
- Integrated fire management
